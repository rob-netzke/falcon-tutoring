About Me

Career History

I began my work as a tutor in late high school when I peer tutored mathematics and writing. In my time at college, I tutored an introductory data science course that taught programming, statistics, and machine learning. During my senior year, I lectured on the course staff as a student instructor. In addition, I tutored part time at Advantage Sideout Tutoring next to Scripps Ranch High School. Upon graduation, I worked as a machine learning engineer until my recent decision to build my own product geared towards practicing data scientists.

Educational Background

I earned two bachelors degrees from The University of California, Berkeley: A B.A. in Data Science and a B.A. in Applied Mathematics. In most of my coursework, I concentrated in economics, but I transitioned my focus to neural networks and machine learning by the end of my senior year. I am a continuous learner, and I am currently focusing on website/app design and deployment to complete my product idea(s).
